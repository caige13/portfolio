About

I'm a software engineer who cares about building things people actually use — and making the people around me more effective. Whether that's shipping production systems, building internal tools that improve a team's quality of life, or creating something a community asked for, the throughline is the same: listen, build, ship, iterate.

At Worlds, I built an internal SDK to streamline how we developed against the platform — centralizing integration patterns and reducing repetitive setup work. At Ethos Group, I was promoted to the R&D team for rapid delivery and led the integration of AI/NLP features across the product suite.

Outside of work, I build for communities I'm part of. AzerothHub started because the WoW community I play in needed a tool nobody had built — now it has thousands of weekly users. PickleBrackit exists because the tournament app our pickleball group used went behind a paywall and organizers needed a free replacement. I listen to what people need and build it.

I'm pursuing an MS in AI at UT Austin while working full-time, and I bring that same energy to everything — collaboration, community, and shipping things that matter.
